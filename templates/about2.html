About Us

Welcome to Chop Shop, an online salon service booking platform! We are a team of enthusiastic students, pursuing Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, working on our TARP project under the guidance of faculties Dr. Suganya G and Dr. Pradeep Kumar.

Our project is an online salon service booking system that allows you to book salon appointments with just a few clicks. But we didn't stop there. We also incorporated dermatologist services into our platform, providing our customers with an all-in-one solution for their beauty and skin care needs.

But that's not all. Our platform also includes a machine learning-based product recommendation system. This system uses your booking history and skin type to recommend personalized beauty products that cater to your unique needs.

We are proud to announce that our project has been developed using Django, a high-level Python web framework. We are confident that our platform will provide a seamless experience for all our customers, and we hope you enjoy using Chop Shop as much as we enjoyed developing it.
